♥ Rule of Thirds

Squarespace

Squarespace.com

The Squarespace website utilizes the rule of thirds by aligning text along the thirds of the page. This balances the structure and draw the user's eye to the important texts without getting lost in the background image.

♥ White Space and Clean Design

Trello

Trello.com

Trello incorperates very colorful and exciitng elements without making it distracting. The balance of white space on the page helps users navigate the page easily and still convey a feeling of calm while adding something fun.

♥ Contrast

Nike

Nike.com

The Nike website exemplifies the design principle of contrast as it often will use bold contrasts between a dark background and a bright text or image to make their products and lifestyle become the focus. This decision on thier website highlights their mission to call people to action through their brand.
