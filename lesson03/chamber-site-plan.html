Windsor Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Cassidy Whitehead

Site Name

The site name will be "Windsor Chamber of Commerce." This site name was selected to be clear and informative to what the website is for.

Site Purpose

The Windsor Chamber of Commerce website aims to serve as a hub for economic growth, community connection, and local business sucess! The website will provide resources to discover more about Windsor. It will have information about community events, programs, and initiatives.

Scenarios

  • What has been the population growth in the area recently?
  • How can I connect with other members of my community through this site?
  • What is an upcoming event that a business owner could involve their business in?
  • How can I contact this chamber for more information?
