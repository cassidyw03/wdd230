About Windsor

The Town of Windsor prides itself on its small-town charm, top-rated programs and events, safety and service. Founded in 1882, Windsor is in the heart of Colorado, with a view of the mountains to the west and the plains to the east.

Statistics

  • 2024 Population Estimate: Approximately 44,769 residents.
  • Growth Rate: From April 1, 2020, to July 1, 2023, Windsor added 7,637 new residents, marking a significant growth rate.
  • Overall Poverty Rate: Approximately 3.62%.
